Built for Transparency, Designed for the Future.

Jhay Banking was created with one goal in mind – to simplify financial management for everyday people. We believe banking should be accessible, transparent, and free from unnecessary complexity. Our mission is to empower individuals with secure financial tools that support growth, stability, and long-term success.

Beyond serving users, this project also doubles as a showcase of my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Each feature and interface has been carefully crafted to practice, experiment, and push the boundaries of web development while delivering a functional banking experience.
